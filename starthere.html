What is Spotify?

Spotify is an online music streaming service. It's like itunes, but better and it's all on the cloud. It is available for desktop computers, as well as iOS and Android smartphones. Music can be browsed or searched for via various parameters, such as artist, album, and genre. Users can also create, edit and share playlists.
